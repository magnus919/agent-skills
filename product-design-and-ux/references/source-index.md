## Source Discipline

1. Record the exact source, version or page status, retrieval date, and the decision it informed.
2. Separate normative requirements, informative guidance, examples, and this skill's synthesis.
3. Verify live status before making a current-standard claim. A rolling page can change without a versioned release.
4. If a source conflicts with user evidence, policy, legal advice, platform behavior, or the supported accessibility environment, preserve the conflict and route it to the accountable owner; do not silently choose the prettier rule.
5. Do not cite a source merely because its vocabulary resembles the decision. Show the link from source scope to the artifact field or acceptance criterion it supports.
