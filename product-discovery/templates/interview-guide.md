## Question Stack

### Warm-up (5 min)
- "Tell me about your role and what you do day to day."

### Process Walkthrough (15 min)
- "Walk me through [specific process] from the beginning."
- Follow-up: "What happens next?"
- Follow-up: "What tells you it's time to start that step?"

### Pain Points / Critical Incidents (15 min)
- "Tell me about the last time this broke down."
- "What's the most frustrating part of this process?"
- "What workarounds have you developed?"

### Latent Needs (10 min)
- Laddering: "Why does that matter?" (repeat 2-3x)
- "What would have to be true for that to work?"

### Assumption Check (5 min)
- Summarize what you've heard and test interpretation
- "What did I miss?"

### Close (5 min)
- "What's the one thing I should have asked but didn't?"
- "Who else should I talk to?"
